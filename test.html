Vikram Singh Bais is an Electrical Engineer by profession with a specialization in Automobiles, farms, and Allied machinery. He has spent his initial 10 years in various FMCG companies holding a leadership position. Seeing the rising demand for Ayurvedic medicines, the Sodhi brothers founded Supherb inc in., in 1988 but could not start the business as the other three brothers were busy in their medical practice. Then in the year 1990, finally Vikram Singh Bais, founder cum Chairman & Managing Director took the challenge to launch the Ayurvedic products of SupHerbs Inc. in India. Simultaneously, he studied Business Management specializing in International Marketing. With his hard efforts SupHerbs Inc: flourished within a short span of three years and become a multimillion and multinational company. It was hard to meet the uprising demand of Indian herbs, for this, a manufacturing unit was set up in India to cater and meet these demands and to ensure quality.

Ayurvedic treatment consists of four basic form:-

  • Medication
  • Panchkarma
  • Dietary Regulation
  • Lifestyle Regulation

Medication is the application of medicines, Dietary regime includes the control of food habits for proper health, Panchkarma is five different karmas or systems of Ayurvedic therapy and Lifestyle regulation is the guideline to control the daily lifestyle. These work as both curative, manageable, and preventive medicines.

Considering the safety of Ayurvedic medicines without side effects, SupHerbs started the acquiring of these medicines firstly in the USA (1988) and to meet the overwhelming demand of our ever-increasing clientele in India in 1994.
